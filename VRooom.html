VRooom!

Unity3D

VRooom! is a Mario Kart inspired karting game built in Unity3D with HTC Vive VR support. You race against bots to see who can get first place while using 5 different power ups to your advantage. You can choose from 3 different karts to drive in VR! This game was built to experiment with Virtual Reality for my Capstone Project at Kennesaw State University.
